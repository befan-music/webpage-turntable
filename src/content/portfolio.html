01

Music Trading Cards

Description

Fans collect items like albums, or songs from booster packs. Artists can claim their account and take control of what Spotify objects are collectible.

Problem

The existing and very popular solution offered by Soundmap excludes artists from the App and is a game without giving users pride in their real listening achievements.

Validation

High frustration in Soundmap community from poor development team with interest for an alternative that would include their favorite artists as platform player.

Solution

Design app as platform to which artists can join in at anytime for free. Connect real listening behavior by awarding 1 booster pack containing 5 items per 1h streaming.

Traction

2 artists with combined 4.5m streams interested and wanted to join if fully rolled out and official. Tight communication with 10 interested users on Discord, yet they require more engaging habit loops beyond MVP status of mere "collectorship"
